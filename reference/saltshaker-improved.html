Apr 29, 2026ABG Now Has an Expert Mode

If the step-by-step guidance has started to feel like training wheels, this update is for you.

What Expert Mode is: You get a blood gas. No steps. No hints. Just the numbers and a text field. Interpret it correctly and move on. Get it wrong and you'll find out.

Why we added it: Advanced users wanted something closer to what it feels like on the wards — ABG in hand, attending waiting, no prompts. Expert Mode is that. The bar is high. No pun intended.

Apr 14, 2026I Fine-Tuned a Language Model on Medical Cases. Here's What Happened.

What if instead of hard-coding decision trees, I trained a small language model to reason through clinical cases the way a physician would? Reader, I tried it.

What I did: Took Llama 3.2 3B Instruct and fine-tuned it on 511 physician-annotated cases from the DiReCT dataset using LoRA, on a free Colab GPU. The loss dropped from 22 to 3.7.

Does it work? The base model confidently claimed cold intolerance comes from too much thyroid hormone. The fine-tuned one correctly identified hypothyroidism, flagged Hashimoto's, and walked symptoms → testing → treatment. Basic, but correct. That's not nothing.

Model is live on HuggingFace if you want to poke at it.
